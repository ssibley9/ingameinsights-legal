Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Derby Hat Contest ("the App") is a party game for running a best-hat contest on a single shared device. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the App to collect as little data as possible.

What the App Accesses

Camera & Photos. The App requests access to your device's camera (or photo library) solely to photograph contestants' hats. These images are used only within the App to display contestants during voting and results.

Contestant Names. You enter a name for each contestant. This is used only for display within the App.

Where Data Is Stored

All data — photos, names, and votes — lives only in the device's memory for the duration of the App session. Nothing is saved to disk, uploaded to a server, or transmitted off the device. When you close the App or tap "Reset Everything," all data is gone.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

Personal information or contact details; location data; device identifiers or analytics; usage or behavioral data; advertising identifiers; any information shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, ad networks, or cloud services. There is no network activity whatsoever.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data leaves the device, there is no risk of unauthorized collection.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to the developer through TestFlight.
