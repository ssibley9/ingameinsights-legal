Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 7, 2026

The short version

Playmaker Lax does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

About This App

Playmaker Lax is a lacrosse play-drawing app developed by InGame Insights LLC. It allows coaches and players to design formations, draw player movement paths, and animate plays on a lacrosse field.

Data We Collect

None. Playmaker Lax does not collect any personal information or usage data. The app does not require an account, login, or any form of registration.

Data Storage

All plays, formation sets, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to InGame Insights LLC or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Playmaker Lax does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads and does not use any third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Because Playmaker Lax does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
