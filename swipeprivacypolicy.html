Overview

SWIPE ("the App") is a multiplayer card game developed by InGameInsights. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the limited data we collect.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following information to enable multiplayer gameplay:

Display Name: The name you enter when creating or joining a game. This is visible to other players in your game session and is not permanently stored.

Anonymous Authentication: The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign a temporary unique identifier to your device session. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity, email, or any account.

Game State Data: During gameplay, information about your cards, scores, and game actions is temporarily stored in Firebase Realtime Database to enable real-time multiplayer functionality. This data is associated with your game room only and is not used for any other purpose.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any of the following: real names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, photos, device identifiers for advertising, health data, financial information, or browsing history.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase for authentication and real-time game data. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Game session data is temporary and is not retained after the game concludes. Anonymous authentication tokens may persist on your device until the app is uninstalled.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
