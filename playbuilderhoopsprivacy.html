# Privacy Policy **PlayBuilder Hoops** Effective Date: March 4, 2026 ## Introduction PlayBuilder Hoops ("the App") is developed by Steven Sibley. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information. The short version: your data stays on your device, and we don't collect any of it. ## Information We Collect **We do not collect any personal information.** The App does not require an account, login, email address, or any form of registration. There are no servers, no cloud storage, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. ## Data Stored on Your Device The App stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework: - **Plays and formations** you create, including player positions, movement paths, passes, annotations, and phase breakpoints - **Roster information** you enter, such as player names and jersey numbers - **Formation sets** you save for reuse All of this data resides exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. ## Data Sharing and Export The App allows you to export plays and formation sets as JSON files and animated videos. These exports are initiated entirely by you and are shared through Apple's standard share sheet. We have no access to exported files or knowledge of when or how you share them. ## Third-Party Services The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud APIs. There are no network requests made by the App. ## Children's Privacy The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. ## Data Retention and Deletion Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete any play, formation set, or roster entry from within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device. ## Changes to This Policy If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted with a new effective date. Because the App does not collect contact information, we are unable to notify you directly of changes. ## Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: **Email:** [support@ingameinsights.com]
