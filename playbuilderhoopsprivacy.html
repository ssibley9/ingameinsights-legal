Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

The short version

PlayBuilder Hoops does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create stays on your device.

About This App

PlayBuilder Hoops is a basketball play-drawing app developed by InGame Insights LLC. It allows coaches and players to design formations, draw player movement paths, animate plays, and export video breakdowns on a basketball court.

Data We Collect

None. PlayBuilder Hoops does not collect any personal information or usage data. The app does not require an account, login, or any form of registration.

Data Storage

All plays, formation sets, roster information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to InGame Insights LLC or any third party.

Data Sharing & Export

The app allows you to export plays and formation sets as files and animated videos. These exports are initiated entirely by you and shared through Apple's standard share sheet. We have no access to exported files or knowledge of when or how you share them.

Third-Party Services

PlayBuilder Hoops does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads and does not use any third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Because PlayBuilder Hoops does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
