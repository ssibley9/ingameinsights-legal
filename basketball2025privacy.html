Basketball 2025 — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Basketball 2025 is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect personal information, require accounts, or use third-party analytics.

Overview

Basketball 2025 is a basketball statistics tracking app for coaches. It records game events, player statistics, and shooting data to help coaches make better decisions. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following: names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising data, or any other personal information. There are no user accounts and no sign-in required.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game data — rosters, player statistics, game events, season records, and team configurations — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage frameworks (SwiftData and UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it.

Device Permissions

Basketball 2025 requests the following device permissions, each of which you can grant or deny individually in iOS Settings:

Permission Purpose Data Handling
Microphone Voice commands for hands-free stat recording during live games Processed by Apple
Speech Recognition Converts voice input into text commands (e.g., "number 23 made a three") Processed by Apple
Local Network Syncs live game data between nearby devices (e.g., a display iPad for spectators) using MultipeerConnectivity Device-to-device only

Audio from voice commands is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition framework and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. Audio is not stored or recorded by Basketball 2025. Local network communication occurs directly between your devices and does not pass through any external server.

Optional Google Sheets Export

The app includes an optional feature to export live play-by-play data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet via Google Apps Script. If you enable this feature, game event data (play type, player numbers, quarter, time, and score) is sent to Google's servers. No personal information is included in this transmission. This feature is entirely opt-in and the app functions fully without it.

Data Export & Sharing

You may choose to export game data as files (CSV or JSON) for your own use. Exported files are created on your device and shared only through actions you initiate (e.g., AirDrop, Messages, email). We have no access to exported files.

Children's Privacy

Basketball 2025 does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not require accounts, does not collect personal data, and contains no advertising or in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

Basketball 2025 does not include any third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, crash reporting SDKs, or tracking technologies. The only external service interaction is the optional Google Sheets export described above.

Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
